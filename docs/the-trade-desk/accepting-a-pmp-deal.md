# Deal Desk: Accepting a PMP Deal in TTD

Below are the steps to accept a PMP deal in The Trade Desk after your Chalice account manager has pushed the deal to your TTD seat.

---

## Step 1. Locate the deal

Open the **Deals Desk** icon on the left side panel. After selecting it, search for the Deal ID provided by your Chalice account manager.

---

## Step 2. Review the deal terms

Open the deal and review the contract terms. If everything looks correct, select **Accept deal**.

---

## Step 3. Select permissions

Choose the desired permission settings.

By default, **Default permissions set by seller** will make the deal accessible to any advertiser within the TTD seat. This is the recommended selection.

!!! tip
    If you want the deal restricted to specific advertisers, select **Override buying permissions set by seller** and choose the appropriate Delivery Profiles.

---

## Step 4. Accept the deal

After selecting the correct permissions, click **Accept deal**.

The deal will now be available to assign within your Ad Groups.
